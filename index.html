A Safe Community
For All of God’s People

At The Kingdom Church, we are more than a congregation—we’re family. This is a house where God’s love runs deep, His truth is preached with power, and every soul is seen. We believe church should be a safe haven—a place where the broken are embraced, the weary are refreshed, and everybody has a seat at the table. No matter your past, your pain, or your process, you belong here. This is holy ground, a sanctuary for healing, growth, and becoming all God has called you to be.

Who We Are

The Kingdom Church is a Pentecostal yet progressive church community committed to transforming lives through the presence and power of God. We are a Christ-centered, Spirit-led ministry passionate about building people and advancing God’s Kingdom in the earth. With a heart for both tradition and innovation, we create space for authenticity, spiritual growth, and generational impact.

What We Believe

We believe in the inspired Word of God as the final authority for life and faith. Our core beliefs are rooted in fundamental Christian doctrine: the Trinity, the deity of Christ, salvation through grace by faith, the power of the Holy Spirit, and the soon return of Jesus Christ. We embrace the gifts of the Spirit, the importance of holiness, and the call to love and serve all people as ambassadors of Christ.

Our Vision and Mission

Our Vision

To see lives transformed, communities empowered, and the Kingdom of God made visible on earth—one soul at a time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to equip believers, reach the lost, and expand God’s Kingdom through Spirit-led worship, sound doctrine, and radical love. We are called to be a voice in this generation, standing on truth, walking in power, and serving with compassion—until earth looks like heaven.

Meet our lead pastor

Pastor Joseph Carr Jr.

Pastor Joseph Carr, Jr. is a Spirit‑empowered leader with a heart for authentic worship, transformative teaching, and Kingdom advancement. Raised in a home of faith, Pastor Joe grew up steeped in Pentecostal tradition and has faithfully shepherded The Kingdom Church since its founding. Passionate about community impact, he bridges timeless doctrine with relevant ministry, empowering people to walk in purpose and faith. Known for his warmth, integrity, and prophetic insight, Pastor Joe leads with unwavering conviction: to see lives liberated, relationships restored, and the Kingdom of God unfold in power.
